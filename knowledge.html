The GAP

MSMEs drives the nation’s GROWTH.

It contributes approximately 8% to India's GDP, employ over 60 million people, have an enormous share of 40% in the exports market and 45% in the manufacturing sector. Hence, they are of paramount importance for overall economic development of India.

While working largely with MSME’s it is realised that the smaller organisations with big visions struggle hard to put things together in the areas of People, Process, Performance & above all Planet during their growth stage.

That is where comes a 4T solution model powered by Technology, Idea and Wisdom named as “PeopleBetter”.

Limitations of MSME’s - People Perspective

Study Says:

More than 93% have limited or zero knowledge of the people importance.

In 20% of cases, business is not clear about the exact role of human capital.

58% of business owners feel that HR function does not need information on the company’s financial statistics.

71% of MSME Business owners opine that HR function are more about employees’ interests and less inclined towards the company’s Goal.

15% of the respondents, HR has two important roles: resolving employee grievances and handling benefits.

In the opinion of 54% of the respondents, staff training is second to other requirements.

67% of the respondents feel that HR is only responsible for recruiting employees. 

34% of business owners believe HR’s primary role is to increase employee efficiency

33 percent feels HR to set goal for business.
